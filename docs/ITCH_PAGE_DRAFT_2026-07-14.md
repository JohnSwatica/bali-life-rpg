## Short description (itch subtitle field)

Arrive in Bali broke. Ride for the app that saved you — until you find
the people it's squeezing. A story-driven delivery-life RPG in one
lantern-lit street of Berawa.

## Page body (draft)

**You step off the night bus with a dead phone, a scam booking, and
Rp 70.** A warung owner you've never met stakes you her late husband's
scooter. The delivery app calls it onboarding. She calls it a debt
between people.

- **Ride.** Continuous steering through storm rain, night lanes, and
  fragile-cargo runs — every delivery is played, never teleported.
- **Talk.** A street of people who remember: the baker who trusts you
  with his name, the fixer who reads due-days not excuses, the rival who
  only respects the leaderboard.
- **Choose.** Keep the mistaken tip or hand it back. Protect a secret or
  open it to the circle. The app scores one of these. The street scores
  the other.
- **Stay.** Crews, sunset circles, a seat that gets made for you without
  asking. Season 1 is the story of earning it.

**Free, in your browser, ~4 hours, phone-friendly.** Season 2 — The
Commission War — depends on you playing this one.
