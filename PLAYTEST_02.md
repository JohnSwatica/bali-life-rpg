Second recorded founder feedback, delivered mid-Wave-1 (after Beats 1–2 of
the Act 1 backbone merged). By CEO authority it pauses deeper story work
until addressed. Raw feedback verbatim per the playtest convention; derived
actions live in `docs/INTERFACE_WORLD_OVERHAUL_2026-07-14.md` and the
amended `LAUNCH_PLAN_SEASON1_2026-07-14.md`.

## Verbatim feedback

> Need to do a phone/shops interface and general visual overhaul before we
> venture deeper into the plots.

## Reading the three problems (CTO annotation)

1. **Task worthlessness** — the phone feed and venue menus still carry the
   pre-pivot generic content (opportunity-engine filler like "Receipt sort
   sprint," "Lost scooter key panic"; venue menus of meter-row activities).
   This is exactly GAME_DESIGN.md §1's failed test: interactions serving
   zero player verbs. The story packets fixed the critical path; the
   *surround* is still the old game.
2. **Interface overload** — 10 phone tabs and multi-section venue panels
   present everything, prioritize nothing.
3. **Lifeless space** — the street and interiors are spacious but empty;
   decoration that exists is noise rather than place-making detail.

→ Wave 1.5 (interface & world overhaul, 4 packets) inserted before Act 1
Beats 3–6. Story work resumes after.
