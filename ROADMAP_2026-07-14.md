## 3. The ending goal

**Launch (Aug 5–12):** a free browser game on itch.io + the existing Pages
URL — a complete 3.5–4.5 hour narrative arc (Acts 0–2 as "Season 1"):
arrive broke → NusaDrop rescue → the app turns → find your people → a seat
at the sunset circle, closed with an authored ending, credits, post-ending
free play, and a Season 2 teaser. Mobile-browser playable, save-safe
(export/import), v1.0 tagged, launch checklist passed on real devices.
The first outside players arrive at launch, by CEO decision — the wave
gates and rubric are the quality floor until then.

**Success at launch is qualitative** (no backend, no analytics by hard
boundary): itch comments/ratings, whether strangers finish the first ten
minutes and say something about a *character* (Kadek/Made/Leo test — if
players mention people, the pivot worked; if they mention meters, it
didn't).

**After launch (not committed, gated on reception):** Season 2 = Act 3
(the café, lifting the management-sim boundary by explicit CEO decision) +
Act 4 (the boycott war with Vance). Act 5 multiplayer/NomadNest tie-in
remains future-only. The stakes-ceiling decision (TODO_LIST.md) governs
whether Season 2 happens at all.
