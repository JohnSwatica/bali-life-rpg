# PLAYTEST_01 — Founder playtest (John), 2026-07-12

The first recorded human play session of Bali Life RPG. ~5 minutes on the
current build (branch `feat/rpg-20260706-09-rio-race`, all 16 packets landed).
Verdict: **bored by minute 5.** This document logs the raw feedback verbatim
per the project's playtest convention; the derived actions live in
`DECISIONS.md` (2026-07-12 entry) and the RPG-20260712 packet queue.

Gate status: this is human feedback #1 of the 3+ GATE v2 asked for. It
redirects the roadmap by CEO authority; outside-player feedback is still
wanted on the post-fix build.

## Reading the four problems (CTO annotation)

1. **Opening** — no cinematic, no plot immersion, no early meaningful choice.
   → RPG-20260712-02 (cinematic cold-open + first-choice beat, built on the
   existing cutscene kit).
2. **No hook in 3 minutes** — the first 10 minutes are walk/click/read/errand.
   → RPG-20260712-02 compresses boot→first timed stakes to under 3 minutes.
3. **Minigame monotony** — everything resolves as a timing-tap variant; wanted:
   continuous obstacle-avoidance steering for deliveries, Diner Dash-style
   service play. → RPG-20260712-03 (steering delivery mode) and -04 (Warung
   Rush). This also matches GAME_DESIGN.md's own L4 priority ("Delivery
   Riding... the single biggest build in the game") — the mandate arrived.
4. **Plot** — fetch-quest chains aren't a story; the three-thread merged bible
   is replaced by the single NusaDrop storyline (STORY_BIBLE v4).
   → RPG-20260712-01 (canon swap + reskin).
